DEMETRES

Design

For this design my aims were to really play around with Demetres themeing and the emotions that their branding ensues on their audiences.

 

The new branding that they have recently published consists of a more refined look but doesn’t lose out on their creativity in their packagings and their menus.

 

Taking inspiration from their summer menu and explosive restaurant renovations, I decided to create an homage to their colourful and delicious brand!
